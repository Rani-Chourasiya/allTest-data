Whiskas (formerly known as Kal Kan) is a brand of cat food sold internationally.
It is available either as small meaty pieces in sauce, gravy or jelly packaged in tins, foil trays or pouches, as well as in the form of small biscuits sold in cartons or pouches and milk sold in small bottles.
Its packaging has a recognisable purple color and a stylized silhouette of a cat's head.
